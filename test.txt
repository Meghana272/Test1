Phase 1: Survey Development and Pilot Testing (2 weeks)

Develop survey questions
Conduct pilot testing
Revise survey based on pilot testing feedback
Phase 2: Participant Recruitment and Data Collection (6 weeks)

Recruit 500 young adults through online platforms and social media channels
Administer survey to participants
Phase 3: Data Analysis and Interpretation (4 weeks)

Clean and analyze survey data using descriptive statistics and regression analyses
Interpret findings and prepare manuscript
Phase 4: Dissemination and Publication (6 weeks)

Submit manuscript for publication in a peer-reviewed journal
Present findings at a relevant conference or seminar.
